Introduction

Welcome to our Spotify clone! This project is designed to mimic the core functionalities of the popular music streaming service, Spotify. Our goal is to provide a similar user experience where you can browse music, create playlists, and enjoy your favorite songs.

This Spotify clone demonstrates the use of modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It features a user-friendly interface, a sidebar for easy navigation, a main content area to explore playlists and songs, and a footer with playback controls.
